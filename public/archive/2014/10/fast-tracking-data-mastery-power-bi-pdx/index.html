Data is changing everything, including what it takes to win in business. Are you ready? Do you feel like you're data-capable? If not, you're not alone. The roadblocks to becoming data-capable are formidable: too much data from too many sources; not enough data understanding on the business side; low quality data or incomprehensible data; limited analysis tool sets.

What does it take to become data-capable? What does that look like and how do you get there as painlessly as possible? Why is Power BI such a smart way to go?

Join CSG Pro to look at a new paradigm for becoming data-capable. We'll talk technology and tools, but we'll be focused on strategy.

• Learn what skill base you need
• Learn what management commitment looks like
•  Learn why (and how) Power BI is a fast track way to get there
•  Understand how to start small for a big payout
